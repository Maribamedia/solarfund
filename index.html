    Mariba Media Disclaimer & Compliance

    Mariba Media (Pty) Ltd (Reg: 2024/132788/07) is an introducing broker with referral arrangements to a network of regulated finance specialists and private lenders. Mariba Media does not hold, process, or transact any funds. It does not provide financial advice, intermediary services, or any other regulated financial service as defined in the Financial Advisory and Intermediary Services Act (FAIS Act, 37 of 2002).

    Mariba Media's only function is to accept client project information and forward that information unchanged to the network of lenders. No curation, matching, feasibility review, recommendation, or filtering of any kind is performed by Mariba Media. All funders are duly licensed credit providers and/or authorised financial services providers. All lending is provided by registered credit providers where required by the National Credit Act (34 of 2005). Mariba Media does not participate in any credit decision.

    Funding terms, credit decisions, due diligence, and FICA compliance are the exclusive responsibility of the lender. No funding outcome is guaranteed.

    We are registered with the Information Regulator in terms of POPIA. Personal information is processed solely for the purpose of forwarding submissions to the lender network. Any data subject may lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator via www.justice.gov.za/inforeg/. All FICA client due diligence obligations are met by the relevant lender, not by Mariba Media.
