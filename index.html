How It Works

Submit Your Project

Your Questions Answered

Complete Transparency

Will I ever be charged a fee? +

No. Our introducing commission is paid entirely by the lender who closes your transaction — and only upon successful deployment. You never receive an invoice from Mariba Funding.

Does Mariba Funding curate or match my deal? +

No. We do not curate, match, filter, or recommend. We forward your submission unchanged to the entire network. If a lender is interested, they will contact you directly.

Is funding guaranteed? +

No. All credit decisions, due diligence, and funding approvals are made exclusively by the lender. Mariba Funding does not participate in any credit decision and provides no guarantee of any outcome.

Who handles FICA and regulatory compliance? +

All FICA client due diligence, credit assessments, and regulatory compliance obligations are the exclusive responsibility of the lender that engages with you. All funders in our network are duly licensed credit providers and/or authorised financial services providers.

How quickly might I hear from a lender? +

Timelines vary by lender and facility type. Some lenders respond within days for invoice discounting or PO funding; larger institutional deals may take longer. We forward your submission promptly — after that, the timeline is between you and the lender.

No curation, matching, feasibility review, recommendation, or filtering of any kind is performed by Mariba Media. All funders are duly licensed credit providers and/or authorised financial services providers. Funding terms, credit decisions, due diligence, and FICA compliance are the exclusive responsibility of the lender that chooses to engage with the client. No funding outcome is guaranteed.
